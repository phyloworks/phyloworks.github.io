Graduate Programs in Biology at ISU

Iowa State offers several interdepartmental graduate programs in biology. In particular, I am interested in recruiting students through the following programs:

  • Bioinformatics & Computational Biology
  • Ecology & Evolutionary Biology

Please contact me if you are interested in joining the lab!

Expectations of Graduate Students

I am looking for independent & creative students broadly interested in theoretical evolutionary biology and phylogenetics. Work in my lab is exclusively computational, but I do not expect incoming graduate students to be expert computer programmers (though that doesn't hurt). When I started my PhD, I had never even considered learning how to program, so I firmly believe that such skills can be gained as part of your training. Thus, I expect that any incoming student will be very keen to learn how to program (in C/C++, python, Java) and make statistical evolutionary biology part of their research.


Although my lab is entirely computational (i.e., "dry"), I am happy to advise students who are also interested in conducing empirical research in addition to computational work. However, I will only be a co-advisor for such students and, if this matches your interests, I encourage you to also contact one of my excellent colleagues in EEOB to discuss the possibility of working with them, too.

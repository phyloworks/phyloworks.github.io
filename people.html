Interested in joining the lab?

I am looking for motivated, independent researchers interested in phylogenetics, macroevolution, molecular evolution, Bayesian inference, statistical modeling, paleontology, computational biology, and any related fields. If you are interested in joining the lab as a graduate student, postdoc, or undergraduate researcher, please contact me to discuss available opportunities.
